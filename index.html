About Me

I grew up in La Grange, a south Chicago suburb. I went to Indiana University for my undergrad to study Finance. After Graduating I moved to Chicago and currently live in Wrigleyville.

I currently work for Oracle as a Sr. Consultant in our Construction and Engineering Global Business Unit. My work entails building and managing our Mid-Atlantic business for a software called Textura. This entails selling and implementing new clients, managing existing clients, and growing our current book of business.

Outside of work some of my hobbies include playing video games, going to concerts and anything to do with sports. During the week I play in a mens basketball league and I'm currently training for the Chicago Marathon. I'm a huge Cubs, Blackhawks, Bears and Bulls fan.
